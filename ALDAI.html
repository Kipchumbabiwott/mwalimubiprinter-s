ALDAI MIXED DAY AND BOARDING PRIMARY & JUNIOR SCHOOL

Aldai Girls Primary School is an ordinary public educational institute in Aldai, Kaptumo, Kenya. This school is managed / sponsored by Anglican Church of Kenya and offers Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE), which is controlled by the Kenya National Examination Council (KNEC) under Ministry of Education.
